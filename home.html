Our Mission

Pedacea is a website that focuses on the day-to-day problems faced by students. The main objective of this website is to provide students a platform to cope up with stress, discover possible career options, manage time efficiently and learn from others’ experiences. Pedacea primarily focuses on students of 17–23-year age group. The website consists of both, front-end and back-end.
